Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total socre to a random number . You lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you clck on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Crystal

Match your points to the random number to win. If you go over, you lose!
